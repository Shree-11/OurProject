About Project

Our system focus on the classification of different ECG samples by comparing existing algorithms and formulating a new approach so as to increase the accuracy and usability. To provide insights of ECG samples of the use, provide symptoms related to the condition and recommend suitable treatments. To analyze the mass ECG samples and to predict the chance of myocardial infarction.

Heart Abnormality Catagories

  • F: Fusion of Ventricular and Normal Beat
  • M: Missing Beat
  • N: Normal
  • Q: Unclassifiable
  • S: Supraventricular Premature Beat
  • V: Premature Ventricular Contraction
